Unique restaurants, mountains and lakes: McCall - Idaho

Baran Manti
Oct 27, 2023

Last week I was in McCall, Idaho to visit my girlfriend. From the first day of my trip, McCall has amazed me with its nature and breathtaking views. I'm a senior student at Michigan State University, so I live in Michigan, and I also have a deep appreciation for Michigan's natural beauty. I'll be sharing more about it in a future post. Living in Michigan means becoming friends with the beautiful fall colors and snow, so spending a week in McCall wasn't very different. However, what sets McCall apart is the air quality and the stunning scenery. Due to its elevation of over 5000 feet, the air feels significantly fresher and crisper. The city itself is situated in a picturesque location right next to Payette Lake. When you stroll through the streets of McCall, you're greeted by the sight of Payette Lake and the mountains that stand behind it. I believe that it makes the whole atmosphere much more friendly and joyful.

McCall also has some pretty good restaurants. Since hunting is so common in McCall, you have a high chance of finding restaurants that offer elk burgers or venison (deer meat). I was quite surprised the first time I saw an elk burger on the menu. As you can probably guess, I gave it a try. It's different from a beef burger but definitely worth trying.

Woods, water and adrenaline: Tippecanoe - Ohio

Baran Manti
Aug 18, 2023

This week, I had the opportunity to spend a week with my cousin's family and my girlfriend in Tippecanoe, Ohio. My cousin and her family live in Cleveland, and I visit them regularly. The kids love me, and they decided to have a fun week together, inviting us to join them in Tippecanoe. Tippecanoe is a beautiful small town located two hours south of Cleveland. Most families in Tippecanoe have been there for generations, and due to this close-knit culture, seeing strangers in their neighborhoods is not common. We found ourselves in a similar situation when we stayed at an Airbnb in a small neighborhood with only twelve houses. The house was right next to a small lake, which was large enough for water sports like water skiing. It seemed like everyone in the neighborhood had their own outdoor toys, such as boats and ATVs.

After seeing that watersports were quite common in the area, we decided to spend the weekend at Tappan Lake. Fortunately, one of my cousin's friends owned a boat, so we could have some fun.
